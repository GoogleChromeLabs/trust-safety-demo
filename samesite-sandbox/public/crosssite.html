Cross-site form submission

  1. This form will submit with a POST request to https://crosssite-sandbox.glitch.me/
  2. https://crosssite-sandbox.glitch.me/ will set cookies then redirect to /redirected.html
  3. /redirected.html should see a request header with cookie: name=value

Open up DevTools on the Network tab. You probably want "Preserve log" checked to see the series of requests.
